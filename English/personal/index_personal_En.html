Natacha Cabellos Ricart. Santiago de Chile.

Lives and works in Valencia, Spain. Visual and media artist. Master in Visual Arts and Multimedia from the Polytechnic University of Valencia, Master in Media Arts from the University of Chile.

Develops his work mainly from the installation and digital environments addressing the negative effects on the global environment caused by human industrial advancement, proposing the study of the plant world to learn new forms of communication, focusing on inter-species collaboration as a strategy.

She has exhibited her work internationally highlighting her participation in Ars Electronica, Campus 2020 Section, Linz, Cuenca Biennial, Ecuador 2020, Lisbon Soa Sound Art Festival 2019, Portugal and Tsonami Sound Art Festival on several occasions. She has received several grants, awards and distinctions, among them the PAM! Award, Valencia Spain (2020), the Chile Crea Grant (2019) and Second Place in the 5th Art and Digital Technologies Competition (2015).
